My Favorite Artists
Artist About Them
Last Dinosaurs An Australian indie rock band consisting of members; Sean Caskey, Lachlan Caskey and Michael Sloane. They formed in 2007 out of Queensland
EDEN An Irish musician named Jonathon Ng. He was formally known as The Eden Project before discontinuing that alias in 2015. Under the alias EDEN, he has released 2 EPs and 3 full length albums.
LOONA A twelve member girl group based in Korea. Each member has their own solo song. They debuted between September 2016 to August 2018. They expanded to Japan in September 2021. They have released 12 solos singles, 2 Japan singles and 6 EPs, 3 of which are from their subunits, 1/3, Odd Eye Circle and YYXY.
Olivia Rodrigo An American singer-songwriter and actress. At just 17, she rose to fame in the music industry with her song drivers license. She later released an album called SOUR, getting her 7 grammy nominations and 3 wins.

What I love about Music

Music has always been an integral part of my life.
There is rarely a moment when I'm not listening to music.
In many of my pivotal moments, I was listening to music or a song from that time brings me back.
Although the songs I've listed here do not have that affect on me, I know in time they will.
Some songs that remind me of specific times of my life are:

  • Times Like These by The Eden Project
  • Calling Out by Amidy
  • Honest by The Neighbourhood
  • start//end by EDEN

These songs, among many others, remind me of the hardships I faced and how I overcame them.
To me, that is the beauty of music. The artist will create with a specific emotion or feeling
but listeners can still make it their own.

My All-Time Favorite Artist

EDEN

Several years ago, I stumbled upon a song called XO by The Eden Project on youtube. I fell in love with it because of its simplicity.
As time went on, I would find other songs by The Eden Project on Youtube and SoundCloud. Then in 2015, he changed his alias to EDEN
and began writing and producing music with a slightly different feel to it. I still loved the music he was putting out so I
continued to follow him and anticipated every release. Since I've been a long-time fan, I feel as if I've grown with him.
All of his music, brings me back to different times in my life. Catch Me If You Can from his first EP as EDEN,
brings me back to my middle school years when I first started to learn about identities and who I might be.
And projector, that brings me back to my second semester in college when I was still at SUNY New Paltz
It was rough adjusting to college life, being away from home and trying to find good people to surround myself with.
I hope as I continue to grow, I will continue to love EDEN's music and let it be an integral part of my life.
